nmap -p 31000-32000 -sV localhost

2. Perform detailed nmap scan on the ports obtained : 
nmap -p 31046,31518,31691,31790,31960 -sV localhost

PORT      STATE SERVICE     VERSION
31046/tcp open  echo
31518/tcp open  ssl/echo
31691/tcp open  echo
31790/tcp open  ssl/unknown
31960/tcp open  echo

3. Now we perform action on ports depending on their types. We find that only port 31790
   is actually useful. And returns us an RSA Key. We can then use this key to connect 
   to our server for bandit17 as shown :
